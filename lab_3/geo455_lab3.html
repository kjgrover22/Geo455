Lab 3: Grover Family Annual Texas Trip

Each year in June around father's day and my birthday, my family loads up our transit van and drives around 1,400 miles to the beach in Texas. We have gotten to know this route and all the cities and communities on the way over the past 15 years, and it has been fascinating to watch it change. Here is that route from my hometown in Minnesota to the beach!

  1. Winona MN
  2. Albert Lea MN
  3. Des Moines IA
  4. Kansas City MO
  5. Witchita KS
  6. Oklahoma City OK
  7. Dallas TX
  8. Waco TX
  9. Victoria TX
  10. Aransas Pass TX
  11. Port Aransas TX
